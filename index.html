Home

I study ice chemistry and radiative transfer in deeply embedded (Class 0/I) protostellar disks, using JWST spectroscopy and RT modeling to disentangle disk vs envelope contributions.

Research snapshot

Focus: spatially resolved ice absorption features (H2O, CO2, CO, CH3OH…) in edge-on systems; model grids to quantify scattering and geometry effects.

  • Current project: Disk vs envelope ice contributions in embedded disks
    Model grids • RT • JWST comparisons
  • Target: IRAS 04302 (“Butterfly Star”)
    NIRSpec + MIRI spatially resolved spectra
